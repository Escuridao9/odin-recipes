Francesinha

Description

The famous Portuguese francesinha. This one is from the city of Porto!

It mostly consists of meats between bread slices covered with a strong sauce.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sausages
  • Cheese
  • Real Meat
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Get some bread
  2. Get some cheese
  3. Cook some meat
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Build the sandwich
  6. Add the cheese on top
  7. Make that sauce drip on the sandwich
  8. Eat it
